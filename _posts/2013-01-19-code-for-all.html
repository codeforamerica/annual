In 2013, in response to massive interest from around the world, we launched Code for All to help organizations everywhere bring the CfA model and mission to their countries. By fostering this network of civic change agents, we are building a global movement for more innovative and open government. Working with our six pilot partners, lessons from the Fellowship, Brigade and Peer Network are being cascaded to local governments worldwide.
