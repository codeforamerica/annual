Brigade

The Brigade is Code for America's network of volunteers who are using their talents to better their communities. In 2013 the Brigade doubled in size, with 3,000+ members and 31 U.S. chapters, and expanded to include international partners. Brigades are maintaining and redeploying apps, lobbying their government to make public it's data, pass data policies, and are building technology. Each Brigade works hand-in-hand with the local government and holds regular events.
