CIVIC TECH MARKETPLACE

The Civic Startups program champions a competitive civic tech market that offers high-quality, high-value tools to government. Civic startups are providing services on top of open data, bringing modern web technology to governments, and changing the way citizens request or receive public services.

For the second year, the Accelerator offered a select group of entrepreneurs training and mentorship from industry leaders. Building on the success of the Fellowship and the Accelerator, CfA created the Incubator to give past Fellows a launchpad to continue their work and scale their CfA projects to new cities. In 2013 three Incubator teams started inspiring new civic tech companies.
