CIVIC STARTUPS

The Civic Startups program champions a competitive civic tech market that offers high-quality, high-value tools to government. Civic startups are providing services using open data, bringing modern web technology to governments, and transforming the way citizens receive public services.

For the second year, the Accelerator offered a select group of entrepreneurs training and mentorship. Building on the success of the Fellowship and the Accelerator, CfA created the Incubator to give past Fellows a launchpad to continue their work and scale their projects. In 2013 three Incubator teams founded new civic tech companies.
