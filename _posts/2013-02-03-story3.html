Having a Voice

textizen.com
‹ ›


Textizen is helping communities from coast to coast lend a listening ear to residents, students, parents, and individuals everywhere who deserve their say. Just this year Textizen worked with Tampa, FL to ask residents whether or not to add an elevated roadway that would reduce traffic congestion and provide evacuation route, worked with Chicago Public Schools to gather input for its 10-year Facilities Master Plan, and helped teach the students of Rex Putnam High School in Milwaukie, OR about mental illness.

Born in the 2012 Fellowship and then expanded as a civic startup in 2013 with the support and guidance of CfA's Incubator program, Textizen is already making strides in civic tech. The team has worked with more than 40 cities and exchanged more than 25,000 text messages.

Textizen is now available in Spanish and will soon launch a new feature, Campaigns, to keep people engaged in ongoing conversation.
